Voice Studio

Create custom voices using Qwen3 TTS. Clone a voice from audio or design one from a text description.

Upload a short audio clip of someone speaking. Qwen3 will learn their voice characteristics and create a new TTS voice. Supports WAV, MP3, M4A, FLAC, OGG, and more.
